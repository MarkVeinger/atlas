### Full Body 5×5
Focus: Full Body · Duration: 65 min · Difficulty: Moderate

Classic 5×5 alternating sessions. Add 2.5 kg to upper body lifts and 5 kg to deadlift each session. One of the most proven strength protocols.

**Equipment:** Barbell, Plates, Rack, Bench

**Tags:** squat, bench, row, deadlift, 5x5, Stronglifts, compound

**Warm-Up**
- Erg or bike — 1 sets | 5 min reps
- Hip circles + shoulder circles — 1 sets | 20 each reps
- Goblet squat — 2 sets | 10 reps | @ 16 kg KB
- Back squat — bar — 1 sets | 10 reps | @ 20 kg — *Find depth and position. Practise the walkout.*
- Build-up set — 1 sets | 5 reps | @ 50% working weight | rest 60s

**Session A**
- Back squat — 5 sets | 5 reps | @ Add 2.5 kg from last session | rest 3:00 — *Full depth. Knees track toes. Brace hard before unracking.*
- Bench press — 5 sets | 5 reps | @ Add 2.5 kg from last session | rest 3:00 — *Shoulders retracted. Bar to lower pec. Drive feet.*
- Barbell row — 5 sets | 5 reps | @ Add 2.5 kg from last session | rest 3:00 — *Row to belly — not chest. Control the eccentric.*

**Session B (alternate)**
- Back squat — 5 sets | 5 reps | @ Same linear progression | rest 3:00
- Overhead press — 5 sets | 5 reps | @ Add 2.5 kg from last session | rest 3:00 — *Standing. Full lockout. No excessive back lean.*
- Deadlift — 1 sets | 5 reps | @ Add 5 kg from last session | rest 3:00 — *1×5 only — it is heavy enough. More sets become junk volume here.*

## Endurance (10 workouts)

### 30-Min Max Distance Test
Focus: Running Test · Duration: 50 min · Difficulty: Max Effort

Aerobic capacity test. Run as far as possible in 30 minutes. Record distance and average HR. Aim for a negative split — second half faster than the first.

**Equipment:** Running shoes, GPS watch, HR monitor

**Tags:** baseline, time trial, aerobic, HR monitor, Cooper test

**Dynamic Warm-Up**
- Easy jog — 1 sets | 5 min reps — *Very easy — wake up the legs only*
- Leg swings — front/back — 1 sets | 10 each leg reps
- Leg swings — lateral — 1 sets | 10 each leg reps
- High knees — 1 sets | 30s reps
- A-skips — 1 sets | 20m reps
- Acceleration strides — 3 sets | 80m reps | rest Walk back — *Build to 85% — fire the fast-twitch fibres*

**Test**
- Max distance run — 1 sets | 30 min reps — *Start controlled at ~80% effort. Increase at the 15-min mark. Final 5 min: everything you have. Record total distance.*

**Cool Down**
- Easy walk or jog — 1 sets | 8 min reps — *Lower HR gradually. Don't sit down immediately.*
- Standing quad stretch — 1 sets | 30s per leg reps
- Hamstring stretch — 1 sets | 30s per leg reps
- Calf stretch — 1 sets | 30s per leg reps
- Hip flexor kneeling stretch — 1 sets | 30s per side reps

### Negative Split Run
Focus: Tempo · Duration: 60 min · Difficulty: Moderate

50-minute progressive run. Every 10 minutes the pace increases. Finishing faster than you started trains the race-specific ability to push when fatigued.

**Equipment:** Running shoes, GPS watch, HR monitor

**Tags:** negative split, tempo, progressive, pacing

**Dynamic Warm-Up**
- Easy jog — 1 sets | 5 min reps
- Leg swings — 1 sets | 10 each leg reps
- High knees — 1 sets | 20s reps
- A-skips — 1 sets | 20m reps
- Calf raise + ankle circles — 1 sets | 15 reps

**Progressive Run**
- Minutes 0-10 — 1 sets | 10 min reps — *Easy — conversational pace. ~9:00-9:30/mi*
- Minutes 10-20 — 1 sets | 10 min reps — *Moderate — breathing harder, still controlled. ~8:30/mi*
- Minutes 20-30 — 1 sets | 10 min reps — *Tempo — comfortably hard. ~8:00/mi*
- Minutes 30-40 — 1 sets | 10 min reps — *Race effort — focused, deliberate. ~7:30/mi*
- Minutes 40-50 — 1 sets | 10 min reps — *Above time trial pace — this is your race. 7:00/mi or faster*

**Cool Down**
- Easy walk — 1 sets | 5 min reps
- Hamstring + calf stretch — 1 sets | 60s total reps
